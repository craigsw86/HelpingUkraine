(What would you be willing to donate to the cause?)

Food All donated Food MUST be sealed. Fresh produce is accepted, but MUST be kept well-preserved during the trip to Ukraine or Poland.
Medical Supplies NO OPENED CONTAINERS OF PILLS OR OTHER MEDICINES!
Computers & Electronics Any donated Computers and other Electronics MUST be wiped clean of all data from previous owner, be in reasonable working condition, and come with power cables.
Clothing Any donated clothing MUST be clean and not have too many holes. NO USED UNDERWEAR; however, we will accept underwear still in the package.
Cars Any donated cars MUST be Diesel and have at least 2.1L tank. Any vehicles with the driving wheel on the righthand side because it comes from a country where people drive on the left side of the road (i.e. the United Kingdom, Cyprus, etc.) are a plus. However, pickup trucks are HIGH PRIORITY because they are multipurpose.
Other Do you have anything else which you would like to donate? Reach out to us to tell us, and we'll let you know if we can use it!

Car4Ukraine These folks have acquired hundreds of cars and fixed them up for battle and evacuations, saving countless lives!
